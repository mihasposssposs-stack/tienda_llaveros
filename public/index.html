About

Who we are and why we make these keychains

About Racing Keychains

We are a small team of motorsport fans and artisans. Each keychain is produced in small runs, painted and finished by hand, then inspected for quality. We focus on faithful color reproduction and durable metalwork so your miniature remains an heirloom.

We offer engraving, secure packaging and tracked shipping. Join our collector community — every release has a limited number of pieces.

Shipping & Returns

Important information about delivery and returns

Shipping & Returns

We ship worldwide. Standard EU shipping: 3-7 business days. International: 7-15 business days. All keychains are shipped with tracking. Returns accepted within 14 days if unused—contact us for instructions.

Packaging: Each keychain includes a protective box and certificate of authenticity.
